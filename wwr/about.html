White Water Rafting Adventures

At White Water Rafting Adventures, our mission is to create unforgettable outdoor experiences that connect people with nature while prioritizing safety, fun, and environmental stewardship. We believe every adventure should leave you with lasting memories and a deeper appreciation for our natural world.

History

Founded in 1998 by river enthusiast Mark Johnson, White Water Rafting Adventures began with a single raft and a passion for sharing the thrill of river running. What started as weekend trips for friends quickly grew into a full-time operation as word spread about our exceptional guides and commitment to safety. Over the past 25 years, we've introduced over 50,000 adventurers to the joy of white water rafting while maintaining our core values of safety first, environmental responsibility, and creating lifelong memories.
